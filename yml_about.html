About Our Program

Empowering young Muslims in Northwest Arkansas to become confident, compassionate leaders rooted in Islamic values and equipped for modern challenges.

Our Mission

The Young Muslim Leadership Program exists to nurture the next generation of Muslim leaders in Northwest Arkansas. We combine Islamic teachings with practical leadership skills to develop well-rounded individuals who can positively impact their communities.

Through engaging workshops, community service projects, and mentorship opportunities, we help young Muslims aged 8-14 discover their potential and develop the confidence to lead with compassion and integrity.
